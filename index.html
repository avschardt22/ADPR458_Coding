About the Pod

Avryn and Gabby met when they were in eighth and ninth grade; now they are best friends and college roommates! As they reach the ends of their college careers, they reflect on their real unfiltered experiences - from making friends and getting involved to discovering what they want to do with their lives.

With humor, honesty and a lot of personal stories, this podcast is a relatable guide for anyone stepping into college or wanting to learn from others' experiences!

Meet the Hosts

Gabby Brady

Gabby is a music educator and percussionist studying at UNL, graduating in May 2025. She's passionate about teaching, performing and leading - whether it's in the classroom, on the UNL Drumline or with any other ensemble or group she's been a part of. She also picks up literally any creative hobby quickly and loves finding, decorating with and crafting with anything green!

Avryn Schardt

Avryn is currently studying advertising and public relations at UNL, graduating in December 2025. She loves playing music with her friends, cheering on the Huskers with the UNL Drumline and playing Fortnite with her little brother. Avryn's favorite time of day is when she can shower and go to bed!
