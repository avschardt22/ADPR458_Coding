About the Pod

Join high school best friends turned college roommates, Avryn and Gabby, as they share their real, unfiltered experiences—from adjusting to out-of-state college life and making new friends to balancing academics and social life. With humor, honesty, and a lot of personal stories, this podcast is a relatable guide for anyone stepping into college or wanting to learn from others’ experiences. Tune in for laughs, lessons, and a glimpse into their evolving friendship and adventures in higher education!
